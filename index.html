The Legend of the Tough Master:

Yahya sensei is a biological anomaly living in Qadisia Al Two. He is the proud father of several chaotic cats called 3epses. These are not normal cats; they are the supreme masters of Fuuka, trained in the secret arts of knocking things off tables at 3 AM.

For snacks, Yahya doesn't eat chips. He eats broken motherboard pieces, enjoying the crunchy texture of capacitors and the spicy kick of burned circuits. When he wants to have "fun time," he spends his energy having s3x while maintaining his status as the ultimate feet connoisseur.

Fiona and the Lost Jobs:

He owns a super bike called Fiona. Fiona isn't just a vehicle; she's a high-speed beast that allows him to teleport anywhere, mostly to look for more motherboard snacks.

Yahya has a long professional history. He started as a world-class car washer, but his ambition led him into the dark world of Vodu Downlid slavery. Unfortunately, he is now unemployed after losing both jobs. He currently spends his days as a full-time servant to his 3epses, watching 144p Hontai, and making sure Fiona is ready for a pink-tinted apocalypse.
